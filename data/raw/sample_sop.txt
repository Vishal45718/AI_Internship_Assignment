TechCorp Product Support — Standard Operating Procedures v2.3
Last Revised: January 2024
Department: Customer Experience

========================================================
SECTION 1: RETURN AND REFUND POLICY
========================================================

1.1 Standard Return Window
Customers may return most products within 30 days of the original purchase date.
To be eligible for a return, the product must be:
  - In its original, undamaged packaging
  - Unused or in the same condition as received
  - Accompanied by the original receipt or order confirmation

1.2 Electronics Return Policy
Electronics have a reduced return window of 15 days from purchase.
All electronic returns must include all original accessories (cables, manuals, warranty cards).
A 15% restocking fee applies to opened electronics unless the product is defective.
Defective electronics are eligible for a full refund or replacement within 30 days.

1.3 Non-Returnable Items
The following items cannot be returned under any circumstances:
  - Downloadable software or digital licenses
  - Perishable goods or consumables
  - Personal hygiene items
  - Custom or personalized orders
  - Items marked "Final Sale" at time of purchase

1.4 Refund Processing
Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.
Refunds are issued to the original payment method only.
Store credit refunds are processed within 24 hours.
International orders may take up to 14 business days for refund processing.

========================================================
SECTION 2: WARRANTY AND REPAIR SERVICES
========================================================

2.1 Standard Warranty Coverage
All TechCorp products include a 12-month limited warranty from date of purchase.
Premium Electronics category products include a 24-month extended warranty.
The warranty covers manufacturing defects and component failures under normal use.

2.2 Warranty Exclusions
Warranty does not cover:
  - Physical damage from drops, spills, or misuse
  - Unauthorized modifications or repairs
  - Normal wear and tear
  - Damage from power surges or improper power supply
  - Water damage beyond rated IP protection level

2.3 Repair Service Process
Step 1: Contact support at support@techcorp.com or call 1-800-TECHCORP
Step 2: Receive a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number
Step 3: Pack item securely and include RMA number on outside of package
Step 4: Ship to designated repair center (address provided with RMA)
Step 5: Receive repair confirmation and estimated completion date (typically 7-10 business days)

========================================================
SECTION 3: CUSTOMER SUPPORT ESCALATION
========================================================

3.1 Tier 1 Support
Handles: General inquiries, order status, basic troubleshooting
Contact: Live chat on website, email response within 24 hours, phone 9AM-6PM EST

3.2 Tier 2 Technical Support
Handles: Complex technical issues, product defects, warranty claims
Contact: Email escalation from Tier 1, scheduled callback within 4 hours

3.3 Tier 3 Engineering Support
Handles: Critical bugs, data recovery, enterprise-level issues
Contact: Escalated by Tier 2 management only
SLA: Initial response within 2 business hours for critical issues

========================================================
SECTION 4: SHIPPING AND DELIVERY POLICIES
========================================================

4.1 Domestic Shipping Options
Standard Shipping (5-7 business days): Free for orders over $50
Expedited Shipping (2-3 business days): $12.99
Overnight Shipping (next business day, order before 2PM EST): $24.99

4.2 International Shipping
International orders ship via DHL Express or FedEx International.
Delivery time: 7-14 business days depending on destination country.
Customs duties and import taxes are the responsibility of the customer.
Free international shipping on orders over $150 USD.

4.3 Order Tracking
All orders receive a tracking number within 24 hours of shipment.
Track orders at: techcorp.com/track or via the email notification sent at dispatch.
