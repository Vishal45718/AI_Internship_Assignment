# TechCorp Product Guide — Getting Started

## System Requirements

### Minimum System Requirements
To run TechCorp software, your system must meet the following minimum specifications:

- **Operating System**: Windows 10 (64-bit), macOS 11.0 Big Sur, or Ubuntu 20.04 LTS
- **Processor**: Intel Core i5 (8th generation or later) or AMD Ryzen 5 3000 series
- **Memory (RAM)**: 8 GB minimum
- **Storage**: 20 GB free disk space (SSD recommended)
- **Graphics**: DirectX 11 compatible GPU with 2 GB VRAM
- **Internet**: Broadband connection (10 Mbps minimum for cloud features)

### Recommended System Requirements
For optimal performance:
- **Processor**: Intel Core i7/i9 or AMD Ryzen 7/9
- **Memory**: 16 GB or more
- **Storage**: 50 GB free space on NVMe SSD
- **Graphics**: NVIDIA RTX 3060 or AMD RX 6600 (4 GB VRAM)
- **Internet**: 50 Mbps or faster

## Installation Instructions

### Step 1: Download the Installer
1. Visit techcorp.com/downloads
2. Log in with your TechCorp account credentials
3. Select your operating system version
4. Click "Download Installer" — file size is approximately 2.3 GB

### Step 2: Run the Installer
1. Locate the downloaded file (TechCorp_Setup_v4.2.exe on Windows, TechCorp_v4.2.dmg on macOS)
2. Double-click to launch the installer
3. Accept the End User License Agreement (EULA)
4. Choose installation directory (default is recommended for most users)
5. Click "Install" and wait for completion (5-15 minutes depending on system speed)

### Step 3: Activate Your License
1. Launch TechCorp after installation
2. Click "Activate License" on the welcome screen
3. Enter your 25-character product key from your purchase confirmation email
4. Connect to the internet for online activation (offline activation available via phone)

## Account Management

### Creating a TechCorp Account
1. Go to techcorp.com/signup
2. Enter your email address and create a secure password (minimum 12 characters)
3. Verify your email address by clicking the link in the confirmation email
4. Complete your profile with your name and country

### Password Reset
To reset your TechCorp account password:
1. Go to techcorp.com/login
2. Click "Forgot Password?" below the login form
3. Enter the email address associated with your account
4. Check your email for a password reset link (valid for 24 hours)
5. Click the link and enter your new password
6. Confirm the new password and click "Save Changes"
7. You will receive a confirmation email when the password change is complete

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
We strongly recommend enabling 2FA for enhanced security:
1. Log in to your account at techcorp.com
2. Navigate to Account Settings > Security
3. Click "Enable Two-Factor Authentication"
4. Choose between authenticator app (recommended) or SMS
5. Follow the setup wizard to complete configuration

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Software Fails to Launch
If TechCorp software fails to launch after installation:
1. Verify your system meets minimum requirements
2. Run as administrator (right-click > Run as Administrator on Windows)
3. Check that your antivirus is not blocking the application
4. Reinstall the Visual C++ Redistributable from Microsoft's website
5. Contact Tier 1 support if the issue persists

### License Activation Fails
If license activation fails:
- Ensure you have an active internet connection
- Verify the product key is entered correctly (no spaces, dashes are included automatically)
- Check that the key hasn't already been used on the maximum number of devices (3 devices per license)
- Contact support with your order number for manual activation

### Performance Issues
If you experience slowness or crashes:
1. Close other applications to free up RAM
2. Update graphics card drivers to the latest version
3. Increase virtual memory allocation in system settings
4. Run the built-in diagnostic tool: Help > Run Diagnostics
