Q: How do I reset my password?
A: To reset your password, visit techcorp.com/login and click "Forgot Password?".
   Enter the email address registered to your account. You will receive a reset link
   within 5 minutes. The link expires after 24 hours. If you don't see the email,
   check your spam folder. If the issue persists, contact support@techcorp.com.

Q: How many devices can I install TechCorp on with a single license?
A: Standard licenses allow installation on up to 3 devices simultaneously.
   Business licenses allow up to 25 devices. Enterprise plans offer unlimited devices.
   To deactivate a device and free a license slot, go to Account Settings > My Devices.

Q: Can I transfer my license to a new computer?
A: Yes. Deactivate the license on your old computer via Account Settings > My Devices,
   then activate it on your new computer using the same product key.

Q: What payment methods are accepted?
A: TechCorp accepts Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, bank transfer (for orders
   over $500), and cryptocurrency (Bitcoin and Ethereum) on select plans.

Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: Log in to your account and navigate to Account Settings > Subscription > Cancel Plan.
   Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period. You will retain
   access to all features until the subscription end date.

Q: What is the difference between TechCorp Pro and TechCorp Enterprise?
A: TechCorp Pro includes all core features, 100 GB cloud storage, and email support.
   TechCorp Enterprise adds unlimited cloud storage, priority phone support, custom
   integrations via API, SSO (Single Sign-On) support, and dedicated account management.
   Enterprise pricing starts at $499/month for up to 50 users.

Q: Does TechCorp work offline?
A: Yes. Core functions work offline. Cloud sync, collaboration features, and AI-powered
   tools require an internet connection. Changes made offline sync automatically when
   you reconnect to the internet.

Q: What file formats does TechCorp support?
A: TechCorp imports and exports: PDF, DOCX, XLSX, CSV, JSON, XML, PNG, JPEG, TIFF,
   and SVG. Native project files use the .tcp format. Third-party integrations support
   additional formats through our API.

Q: Is there a mobile app?
A: Yes. TechCorp Mobile is available for iOS (iOS 14 or later) and Android (Android 9 or later).
   The mobile app supports viewing and light editing. Full feature parity with desktop is
   planned for Q3 2024.

SECURITY & PRIVACY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Q: How is my data protected?
A: All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption and in transit using TLS 1.3.
   TechCorp is SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. Our servers are hosted in
   AWS data centers in the US and EU with 99.9% uptime SLA.

Q: Can TechCorp staff access my files?
A: No. TechCorp uses zero-knowledge encryption for personal files. Our staff cannot
   access your file contents. Support staff can only see account metadata (email,
   subscription status) necessary to resolve support tickets.

Q: How do I enable two-factor authentication?
A: Go to Account Settings > Security > Two-Factor Authentication. We support
   authenticator apps (Google Authenticator, Authy, 1Password) and hardware keys
   (YubiKey). SMS-based 2FA is available but not recommended due to SIM-swap risks.

Q: Why is my cloud sync not working?
A: Common causes: (1) No internet connection, (2) Sync is paused — check the sync icon
   in the system tray, (3) Storage quota exceeded — upgrade your plan or delete old files,
   (4) Firewall blocking TechCorp on ports 443 and 8080.
   Run Help > Run Diagnostics to identify the specific issue.

Q: I accidentally deleted an important file. Can I recover it?
A: Yes! Deleted files are kept in the Trash for 30 days. Go to the Trash section to
   restore them. Pro and Enterprise users also have File History, which keeps previous
   versions of files for up to 90 days. Contact support immediately for deleted files
   older than 30 days — recovery may still be possible for 14 additional days.

Q: The application is running slowly. What can I do?
A: Try these steps: (1) Restart the application and your computer, (2) Clear the
   application cache via Settings > Advanced > Clear Cache, (3) Ensure you have at
   least 4 GB of free RAM while TechCorp is running, (4) If using a large project
   (over 10,000 items), enable Performance Mode in Settings > Performance.
